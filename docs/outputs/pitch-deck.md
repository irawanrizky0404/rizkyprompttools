# Pitch Deck: Lumina — AI-Powered Event Photography

## Slide 1: Title
> **Lumina**
> Real-time AI event photography — no uploads, no waiting.
> *"Your guests get pro photos before the canapés get cold."*

## Slide 2: Problem
- Event photographers charge $3k–$10k per event
- Guests wait 2–4 weeks to receive edited photos
- 73% of event attendees never post photos because they don't like how they look
- Venues lose viral social media exposure

## Slide 3: Solution
Lumina cameras + AI process photos on-device in < 3 seconds:
- Auto-crop, color-grade, remove red-eye, smooth skin
- Guests scan QR code → view + download instantly
- No app install, no account creation

## Slide 4: Market Size
| Segment | TAM | SAM | SOM |
|---------|-----|-----|-----|
| Weddings | $4.2B | $1.1B | $45M |
| Corporate events | $2.8B | $600M | $22M |
| Nightlife | $1.5B | $400M | $18M |

## Slide 5: Business Model
- **Hardware**: $299/camera (BOM $112, margin 62%)
- **Software**: $99/event SaaS fee per camera
- **Prints**: Revenue share with on-site printing partners (30% margin)
- **Enterprise**: $5k–$15k/year for venues with fleet management

## Slide 6: Traction
- 12 beta events completed (3 weddings, 7 corporate, 2 concerts)
- 8,400 photos processed, avg 4.7★ rating
- LOIs from 3 venue chains (22 locations total)
- Pre-orders: 47 units at $299 deposit
