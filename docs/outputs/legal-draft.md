# Legal Document Draft: Software as a Service Agreement

---

**PARTIES:**
1. **CloudSync Inc.** (Company) — 100 Market St, San Francisco, CA 94105
2. **Customer** — As identified in the Order Form

**EFFECTIVE DATE:** March 15, 2025

---

## 1. DEFINITIONS
- **"Services"** means the CloudSync platform, APIs, and all associated features.
- **"Order Form"** means a document executed by both parties specifying subscription tier, term, and fees.
- **"Confidential Information"** means non-public technical, business, or financial data.

## 2. SERVICES & SUPPORT
2.1 Company will provide Services materially consistent with the Documentation.
2.2 Uptime SLA: 99.9% monthly. Each 0.1% below = 5% service credit (max 30%).
2.3 Support: Standard (9–5 PT, 4hr response) or Premium (24/7, 30min critical response).

## 3. FEES & PAYMENT
| Tier | Price | Users | Storage | API Calls/mo |
|------|-------|-------|---------|-------------|
| Starter | $99/mo | 10 | 50 GB | 10k |
| Pro | $299/mo | 50 | 250 GB | 100k |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited | 2 TB | 1M+ |

Payment terms: Net 30. Late payments: 1.5% monthly interest.

## 4. DATA PROTECTION
4.1 Company is SOC 2 Type II certified. Audit report available under NDA.
4.2 Data hosted on AWS us-west-2, with hourly backups to us-east-1.
4.3 Customer Data remains Customer's property. Company may access only to provide Services.
4.4 DPA attached as Schedule A. GDPR-compliant processing terms included.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
5.1 Company retains all IP in the Services, including improvements.
5.2 Customer retains all IP in Customer Data uploaded to the platform.
5.3 Feedback provided by Customer may be used freely by Company.

## 6. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
6.1 Neither party's aggregate liability exceeds fees paid in the 12 months preceding the claim.
6.2 Exclusions: no liability for indirect, consequential, or lost profits.
6.3 Exceptions: confidentiality breaches, IP infringement, indemnification obligations.

## 7. TERM & TERMINATION
7.1 Initial term: 12 months. Auto-renews annually unless 30-day notice given.
7.2 Either party may terminate for material breach uncured within 30 days.
7.3 Upon termination, Customer has 60 days to export data. After 60 days, Company may delete.

## 8. INDEMNIFICATION
Company will defend Customer against third-party IP claims alleging the Services infringe a patent, copyright, or trade secret.
